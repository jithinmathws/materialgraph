Design boundary:
MaterialGraph compares existing deterministic pathway opportunities.
It does not replace scientific judgement or experimental validation.

Known next-stage limitation:
Scientifically distinct endpoint opportunities may receive identical pathway
scores when existing ranking dimensions are identical. Endpoint-sensitive
differentiation belongs in the ranking intelligence layer, not the
comparative intelligence layer.
